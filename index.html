About:

My name is Nick and I am a Web Developer proficieint in Javascript, HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, and NodeJS. Before beginning a career in web development, I worked as a music producer and educator. As a producer, I developed strong leadership, communication, and organizational skills. These skills enable me to productively express, share, take in, and postivily critique ideas within a team. I'm passionate about music production, but found a desire to expand my skill set so to more effectively share that information with other people. This led to enrolling in the University of Pennsylvania's LPS Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp. I found web development required similar problem solving and team-management skills I utilized as a producer. I have grown to enjoy the possibilities web development offers in terms of growth, sustainability, and career development. I look forward to being part of a community that offers growth and opportunity when challanges are met and accomplished.
